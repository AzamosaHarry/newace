Engr. Christopher Nwokolo

- Group CEO

Engr. Christopher Nwokolo is an accomplished Chartered Petroleum Engineer with a wealth of experience in the energy sector, spanning over 20 years. He is a member of the Energy Institute UK, which is a testament to his industry expertise and contributions to the sector. Christopher has an impressive track record of delivering results in various areas of the industry, and his areas of expertise include Gas Infrastructure Development Financing, development of organic gas value chains, Gas Commercialization, GHG Emissions Management, Gas Operations and Engineering, Integrated Project Management for oil and Gas Field Development, Front End Development Studies for Oil and Gas Field Development, Upstream and Midstream Business Opportunity Management, Business Development and Strategic Business Intelligence and Advisory for Upstream/Midstream oil and gas asset development and management.

Christopher's passion for energy has made him a trusted advisor to some of the industry's leading players. He began his career with Chevron Nigeria Limited as a Base Business Engineer, focusing on Corporate Asset Management, before moving on to Shell, where he gained extensive experience in upstream operations, engineering, and project management. He later joined Seplat Energy, where he held various leadership roles, including Gas Operations Interface Manager and Gas Business Opportunities Manager. During his time at Seplat Energy, Christopher played a significant role in driving the company's growth and expansion into new markets, developing new business models, and establishing strategic partnerships. He is on the board of several Upstream and Midstream E&P companies and leads the pack in midstream gas solutions and deployment of FLNG Gas Technologies in Nigeria.

In addition to his sterling career, Christopher has also held several leadership positions with SPE Lagos and sits on the council of the Energy Institute, Nigeria. These positions reflect his dedication to advancing the industry and his commitment to the development of the energy sector in Nigeria and beyond.

With a degree in Petroleum Engineering from the University of Benin and a Master's Degree in Reservoir Evaluation and Management from the Institute of Petroleum Engineering at Heriot-Watt University, Christopher has a strong technical background and a deep understanding of the engineering principles that underpin the energy industry. He is passionate about exploring new technologies and finding innovative solutions to industry challenges. Christopher is an excellent communicator and has exceptional interpersonal skills, building strong relationships with stakeholders at all levels. He has authored several technical papers and won several awards, reflecting his contributions to the sector.

As a petroleum engineer, Christopher has a strong technical background and a deep understanding of the engineering principles that underpin the energy industry. He is passionate about exploring new technologies and finding innovative solutions to the challenges facing the industry. He has authored several technical papers, showcasing his expertise and contributions to the sector and has won several awards.

In addition to his work in the energy industry, Christopher is also involved in various philanthropic and community development initiatives, reflecting his commitment to making a positive impact on society. Christopher's dedication to the energy sector and his commitment to sustainable and innovative solutions make him a highly sought-after expert in the industry.


Collins Oguike

- VP, Business Development & Strategy

Collins Oguike is a seasoned geologist and environmental conservationist with over 15 years of experience in business operations, business development and strategy, corporate finance, and advisory. He is a highly skilled and experienced professional with a proven track record of success in the oil and gas industry. Collins has a deep understanding of the industry, having worked for industry giants such as Schlumberger and Halliburton, where he honed his skills and expertise.

Collins Oguike is a graduate of the University of Jos, where he earned a bachelor’s degree in Geology and Mining. He is a Chevening Scholar with an Msc in Environmental Conservation from the University of Greenwich, attended the French Institute of Petroleum and also a product of the prestigious Lagos Business School on African Leadership, where he developed his strategic thinking, business development, and financial management skills including sustainable socio-economic growth with the environment as a vital capital.

Collins has a strong passion for developing and executing innovative business strategies that drive growth and sustainability. His expertise in ESG, business development, corporate finance, and business operations has made him an invaluable asset to various organizations he has worked with. He has a keen eye for identifying emerging opportunities, mitigating risks, and developing strategies that align with organizational goals.

Collins Oguike is a visionary leader who inspires excellence in his team and drives results through a culture of collaboration, innovation, and excellence. He has a reputation for delivering exceptional results, and his commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and admiration of his colleagues and peers in the industry. He currently sits in the board of several companies in the Oil and Gas space as well as in some fin-tech and agro-allied companies.

As a seasoned geologist, mining engineer and environmentalist, Collins understands the importance of sustainability and environmental responsibility in the oil and gas industry. He is committed to delivering sustainable solutions that promote growth and progress while ensuring that the environment is protected. Collins is a leader who is dedicated to creating value for stakeholders and driving positive change in the industry.
