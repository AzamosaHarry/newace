Who We Are

Welcome to Ace Gas & FLNG Company Limited, a leading gas and power company committed to delivering innovative energy solutions to meet the world's energy demands. Our company, a subsidiary/Consortium member of Cratos/TransOceanic Energy Group, was founded on the values of excellence, integrity, and sustainability and has remained a forefront player in the gas and energy industry for several years.

At Ace Gas & FLNG Company Limited, we offer a wide range of services to our clients. We specialize in financial and technical services agreements for upstream oilfield developments, which help our clients maximize their resources and achieve their production goals. Our expertise in decarbonization, flare solutions, and carbon credits allows us to help our clients reduce their environmental footprint and contribute to a sustainable future.

We also specialize in midstream solutions for gas development, oil field produced water management, and engineering design and procurement of static and rotating equipment, including gas compressor stations, power generation plants, and oil and gas processing equipment. Our team of experts has extensive knowledge in designing and deploying advanced flare solutions, such as incinerators and thermal oxidizers.

At Ace Gas & FLNG Company Limited, we are dedicated to promoting sustainable development in Nigeria's energy sector. We work closely with all our stakeholders, including host communities, government agencies, and regulatory bodies, to ensure that our operations are carried out in an environmentally responsible and socially conscious manner.

Our integrated gas and power solutions are tailored to meet the varying needs of our customers, from large industrial users to households and small businesses. We invest heavily in technology and innovation to ensure that our products and services remain at the forefront of the industry.

Additionally, we provide life extension services for aging facilities, offshore marine engineering solutions, EPF and advanced well testing solutions, refining solutions, and design and deployment of land and marine crude oil and petroleum product storage facilities. Our unwavering commitment to excellence in every service we offer has earned us a reputation as one of the most reliable names in the oil and gas industry.

Ace Gas & FLNG Company Limited is proud to be a Nigerian-owned and operated company, and we are committed to contributing to the growth and development of our nation. We prioritize building strong relationships with our customers and stakeholders based on trust, integrity, and mutual respect.
